InstructionsStep 2 of 6
How to Complete This Form

Please help us improve our classes by evaluating your teacher.

  1. 1Write your teacher's name in the space provided on each evaluation form.
  2. 2Read each category carefully before giving a score.
  3. 3Give a score from 0 to 100 for each category based on your experience.
  4. 4Be honest and fair when giving your score — your feedback helps teachers improve.
  5. 5You may write a short comment, suggestion, or recommendation for your teacher at the bottom of each form.

CategoriesStep 3 of 6
Evaluation Categories

Here is what each category means — read carefully before scoring.

1. Teacher Preparation

  • The teacher is ready for class.
  • The teacher starts class on time.
  • The lesson is organized.

2. Teacher Explanation

  • The teacher explains clearly.
  • The teacher gives good examples.
  • The teacher helps me understand my mistakes.

3. Understanding

  • I can understand the teacher.
  • The teacher speaks clearly.
  • The teacher speaks at a good speed.

4. Assistance

  • The teacher answers my questions.
  • The teacher helps me when I have problems.

5. Support

  • The teacher is kind and patient.
  • The teacher makes me feel comfortable in class.
